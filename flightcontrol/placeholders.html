FlightControl Placeholders

Plugin Page

You MUST be using either PlaceholderAPI or MVdWPlaceholderAPI for these placeholders to work.
Those plugins use different types of characters to surround their placeholders
(PlaceholderAPI: %flightcontrol_tempfly_short%, MVdW: {flightcontrol_tempfly_short}).
MVdWPlaceholderAPI only works if you have one of their premium plugins bought,
so I suggest using PlaceholderAPI if you have not already purchased one of MVdW's plugins.
Join the Support Discord if you need help.

flightcontrol_tempfly_shortReturns the duration of tempfly left in shortened format (e.g. 4m 3s)
flightcontrol_tempfly_longReturns the duration of tempfly left in normal format (e.g. 4 minutes, 3 seconds)
flightcontrol_tempfly_sReturns the duration of tempfly left in seconds
flightcontrol_tempfly_mReturns the duration of tempfly left in minutes
flightcontrol_tempfly_hReturns the duration of tempfly left in hours
flightcontrol_tempfly_dReturns the duration of tempfly left in days
flightcontrol_flyingReturns if a player is flying as a boolean ("true" or "false")
