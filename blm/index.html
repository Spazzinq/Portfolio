CONTACT

Send e-mails and phone in. Let local/state governments know that you care.
Find the local authority: https://myreps.datamade.us/...

You can urge...
  • ✅ congressmen/women to co-sponsor the House Resolution condemning police brutality,
  • ✅ local attorneys to arrest and prosecute the offending officers (if they actually have the power),
  • ✅ ???? to defund the local police department,
  • ✅ ???? to divert funds to Black/in-need communities for education, health and safety programs.

Example e-mail about George Floyd:
Hello (AUTHORITY),

My name is (NAME) and I am a resident of (STATE/CITY). I am emailing today to demand accountability and justice for the racist murder of George Floyd. I demand that charges be pressed against all officers involved in this unjustified murder, including specifically Derek Chauvin and Tou Thao. They should not be allowed to keep their jobs and should be charged and prosecuted to the fullest extent of the law for manslaughter. George Floyd would be alive today if it was not for the gross abuse of power and white supremacy exhibited by the Minneapolis Police Department. All officers involved must face consequences for this murder in order to provide his family with justice and prevent further brutal acts of violence from cops against our communities. In addition, I demand that we provide more support towards communities and organizations outside of the police forces in order to prevent police brutality and violence in the future.

Sincerely,
(NAME)
